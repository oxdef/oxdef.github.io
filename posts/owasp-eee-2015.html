OWASP EEE - Russia event call for speakers

Posted on 14 September 2015 in misc

OWASP EEE, October 6-12 2015 7 days, 1 virtual AppSec event, 7 local events streamed globally online. Participating chapters: Poland, Lithuania, Cluj, Bucharest, Hungary, Russia, Austria.

Through 7 consecutive days we will organize local mini-conferences devoted to application security. Lectures will differ at each event so this is not a road show where all events are the same and repeated. All the speeches will be streamed live on the Internet and will remain available on our youtube channel.

We want to take advantage of streaming and reach to as broad audience as possible, using both global OWASP channels and local relationships. This initiative will enable participants to get the most out of OWASP events and meetings in the region.
