OWASP Russia Meetup 3

Posted on 07 June 2015 in misc

This time meetup took place in Mail.Ru office. We talk about XPath/XQuery injections, deeply about CSP and other web application security technologies. All slides will be available soon on slideshare.net/OWASP-Russia. My talk was titled "Web Application Security: future standards and technologies" and it was the selection of work projects of W3C Web Application Security Working Group for 2015. Stay tuned: subscribe to our mailing list and follow us on Twitter @owasp_ru.
